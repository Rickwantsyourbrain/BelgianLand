About Us

PHILOSOPHY

GOVERNANCE

Belgian land focuses on the development of aﬀordable housing in Belgium in partnership with real estate developers.

Belgian Land is a limited liability company ("SA") under Belgian law incorporated by Bank Degroof-Petercam and the real estate advisor CBRE.

The activities of Belgian Land started in March 2013, pursuant to the capital investment of almost 55 million euro through private placement.

This initiative is based, on the one hand, on the acknowledgment of the strong economic, social and demographic evolution in most of the large urban areas in Belgium and, on the other hand, on the many development opportunities for which a large number of developers lack the ﬁnancial means.

The strategy of Belgian consists of investing in aﬀordable residential development projects in partnership. Projects of interest will be those targeting a population with average purchasing power in the large cities.

Belgian Land is also taking a position as an active partner in each project. With an operational team, Belgian Land leads with his partners, the development of their projects, from the conception to the sale of the last unit.

Glacière : Consultation Committee for our project in Auderghem

By Olivia Jean

The consultation committee for the project Glacière, for which a permit was introduced end 2015, was held today in the city Auderghem. The Glacière project is a mixed project of 36,000m² consisting of housing, student rooms and retail.

This consultation committee has focused on the speciﬁcations of the impact study.The administrative procedure is put forth and permits are expected mid-2018.
